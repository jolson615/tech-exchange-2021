About Us

Mariah began her book adventure on Instagram where she focused on creating baked goods to match what she was reading.

As the world emerged from the COVID-19 pandemic, she was able to open her bookstore bakery dream and become the second working bookstore in the Bronx!

Today, we offer books and baked good sales - matching or not. Come in and pay us a visit, the whole team is happy to help!
